Django Developer Needed

We are looking for a django developer for coralnet.ucsd.edu.

CoralNet is a data-annotation service that allows coral ecologists to process their image data fast and accurately. The site deploys state-of-the-art computer vision methods, and is used by researchers and government agencies across the world.

We are looking for a part-time developer with django experience to help take the site to the next level. This includes porting it to AWS, deploying a new deep-learning based vision backend, and adding new features. You would formally be working for Prof. David Kriegman at UCSD but your day-to-day activities would be supervised by Oscar Beijbom.

The position offers:

  • Flexible work hours and competitive salary.
  • Oportunity to work on a real site with real impact.
  • Exposure to the latest and greatest in deep learning.

If interested please contact Oscar at obeijbom@eecs.berkeley.edu. Please include brief resume and a link to previous projects in your application.
